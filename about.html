About me & this blog

Hi! I'm Emily — a graduate student in biostatistics at Harvard, currently cross-registered in MIT's Healthcare Ventures class. I spend my days working with data, statistics, and code.

This blog is where I document what I'm learning and building. It started as a class assignment, but I'm using it as a space to share projects, notes, and the occasional deep dive into topics I find interesting.

When I'm not staring at R or SAS output, you might find me exploring new datasets, reading papers, or debugging something that "should definitely work."
